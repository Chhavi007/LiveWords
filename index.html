about us

why choose us?

Learn as You Type, Anywhere Online

A browser extension designed to seamlessly integrate into users' digital experiences and facilitate language learning. By tapping into automatic translation technology, the extension will enable users to effortlessly view translations in their target language as they read or type in their native language. This eliminates the need for manual translation, making it easier for learners to immerse themselves in the new language. With this handy tool, individuals can enhance their language skills naturally and effectively, seamlessly incorporating learning into their daily routine.
